Who Are We?




Kirala Gitsin,it is a subscription system where you can rent the product you want for the monthly rental price for the rental period you need.

Kirala Gitsin's Privileges


Wallet Friendly

With KiralaGitsin, only pay for what you use.Rent tech for as long as you need and return it free of charge.

Worry Free

Relax knowing 90% repair or replacement coverage (Kirala Gitsin Care) is included in your rent.

Stay Flexible

At the end of the rental period, you can keep renting, send it back for free, or buy at any time.
